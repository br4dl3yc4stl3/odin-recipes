French Toast Recipe

Description

French toast is a staple of the breakfast menu. The dish can be made with only a few ingredients, and be ready to eat in less than 30 minutes. French toast tastes delicious when served with fresh fruit of your choice and maple syrup.

    Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk (or Milk substitute)
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract

    Steps: (meal for 1 Person)

  1. Get out a large bowl
  2. Crack 2 eggs open and put inside bowl
  3. Pour 3/4 cup of milk or substitute inside bowl
  4. Put 1 tbsp of cinnamon in bowl
  5. Put 1 tbsp of vanilla extract inside bowl
  6. Mix all ingredients together
  7. Dip bread slice into bowl
  8. Place dipped bread slice onto pan
  9. Heat pan on Medium and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  10. Enjoy!
